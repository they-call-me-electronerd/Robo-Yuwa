Frequently Asked Questions

Are the programs really free?

Our workshops are completely free for students. Structured courses have a nominal fee to cover materials, but scholarships are available for deserving students.

Do I need prior experience?

No prior experience needed for beginner programs! Each program has prerequisites listed to help you choose the right level.

What equipment do I need?

We provide all necessary hardware during workshops and courses. You'll just need a laptop for programming exercises.

How do I register?

Visit our Events page for upcoming workshops or contact us to apply for courses and mentorship programs.
