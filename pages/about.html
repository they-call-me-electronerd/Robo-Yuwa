About RoboYuwa

Empowering the next generation of innovators

Our Mission

To democratize STEM education across Nepal by providing accessible, hands-on learning experiences in Robotics, Artificial Intelligence, and Internet of Things to students from all backgrounds.

Our Vision

A future where every Nepali youth has the skills, confidence, and opportunities to innovate and lead in the global technology landscape.

Our Story

RoboYuwa was founded in 2020 by a group of passionate engineers and educators who recognized the growing gap between technological advancement and accessible STEM education in Nepal.

What started as weekend workshops for 20 students has grown into a nationwide movement, reaching over 500 students across multiple cities. We've conducted 30+ workshops, completed 50+ student projects, and built a community of mentors, volunteers, and supporters.

Today, RoboYuwa continues to expand its reach, offering comprehensive programs that combine theoretical knowledge with practical, project-based learning.
