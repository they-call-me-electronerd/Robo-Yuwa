**RoboYuwa** is a pioneering youth-led, non-profit organization dedicated to robotics, artificial intelligence, and Internet of Things (IoT) innovation in Nepal. ### Our Name
The name "RoboYuwa" combines:
- **Robo** - Representing robotics and technology
- **Yuwa** - The Nepali word for youth

This perfectly encapsulates our core identity as a youth-driven technology initiative.
