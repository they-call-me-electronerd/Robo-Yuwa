About RoboYuwa

Inspiring Nepal's youth to become tomorrow's innovators through hands-on technology education

Our Mission

Empowering the Next Generation

RoboYuwa is dedicated to democratizing access to cutting-edge technology education across Nepal. We believe every young person deserves the opportunity to explore robotics, artificial intelligence, and the Internet of Things.

Through hands-on workshops, mentorship programs, and community projects, we're building a generation of problem-solvers, innovators, and leaders who will shape Nepal's technological future.

  • Provide accessible, high-quality tech education to all students
  • Foster innovation and creativity through project-based learning
  • Build a supportive community of learners and mentors
Our Vision

A Tech-Enabled Nepal

We envision a Nepal where every student has the skills, confidence, and resources to harness technology for positive change. A nation recognized globally for its technological innovation and talented tech workforce.
