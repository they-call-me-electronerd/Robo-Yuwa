About Us

Empowering Youth Through Technology

RoboYuwa is a pioneering youth-led, non-profit organization dedicated to robotics, artificial intelligence, and Internet of Things (IoT) innovation in Nepal. Founded in 2024, we emerged from a shared vision among young technology enthusiasts who recognized the need to bridge the gap between traditional education and the rapidly evolving world of technology.

The name "RoboYuwa" combines "Robo" (representing robotics and technology) with "Yuwa" (the Nepali word for youth), perfectly encapsulating our core identity as a youth-driven technology initiative. We empower young minds with cutting-edge technology and foster innovation through hands-on learning and community collaboration.

We envision building a thriving community of young innovators who will shape Nepal's technological future and contribute meaningfully to the global tech ecosystem.
